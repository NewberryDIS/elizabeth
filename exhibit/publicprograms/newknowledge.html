Elizabeth I: New Knowledge and Old Mysteries

Saturday, January 17, 11:00 a.m.
The Newberry Library

Speaker: Clark Hulse, University of Illinois at Chicago
Four hundred years after her death, Queen Elizabeth I still inspires both scholarly and popular imagination. Join exhibit curator Clark Hulse for a discussion of the exhibit and recent discoveries about the life and reign of this enigmatic Queen.

Admission is free. No reservation required.
