Elizabeth I: Ruler and Legend
CHRONOLOGY OF ELIZABETH'S LIFE & TIMES

1510 Henry VIII becomes king of England and marries Katherine of Aragon
1516 Queen Katherine gives birth to Mary Tudor
1517 Martin Luther begins Reformation at Wittenberg, Germany
1533 Henry VIII divorces Katherine of Aragon and marries Anne Boleyn
Henry VIII breaks with the Roman Catholic Church
Queen Anne gives birth to Elizabeth Tudor (Sept. 7)
1536 Queen Anne is executed
1537 Queen Jane Seymour gives birth to Edward Tudor
1547 King Henry VIII dies
Edward VI becomes king
1553 King Edward VI dies
Mary Tudor becomes queen and restores Catholicism
1554 Elizabeth is imprisoned
Queen Mary I weds King Philip II of Spain
1558 Queen Mary I dies
Elizabeth Tudor becomes queen
1568 Mary Queen of Scots takes refuge in England
1569-71 Northern Rebellion in Yorkshire is suppressed
1570 Pope Pius V excommunicates Elizabeth from the Roman Catholic Church
1572 Thomas Howard, Duke of Norfolk, is executed for his role in the Ridolfi Plot
Saint Bartholomew's Day Massacre kills over 100,000 Protestants in France
1577-80 Sir Francis Drake circumnavigates the globe
1584 Sir Walter Raleigh establishes the colony of "Virginia," named for the Virgin Queen
1586 The Babington Plot, a conspiracy involving Mary Queen of Scots and her page Anthony Babington, is revealed
1587 Mary Queen of Scots is executed
1588 The English navy and foul weather defeat Philip II's Spanish Armada
1594 William Shakespeare and others form the Lord Chamberlain's Men
1601 Robert Devereaux, Earl of Essex, leads a rebellion and is executed
1603 Queen Elizabeth I dies (Mar. 24)
James VI of Scotland becomes King James I of England
